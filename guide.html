EthicalUX - Extension Guidelines

Welcome to our EthicalUX - Extension. Our mission is to help users identify and understand dark patterns on the web, promoting a more transparent and user-friendly online experience.

Guidelines

  • Be aware of misleading design elements that trick users into unwanted actions.
  • Identify patterns that create a sense of urgency to manipulate user decisions.
  • Recognize interfaces that make it challenging for users to opt-out of certain actions.
  • Report instances of hidden costs, subscriptions, or other deceptive practices.
  • Share your findings and contribute to raising awareness about dark patterns.

Reporting

If you come across a website employing dark patterns,contact us to share your observations. Together, we can make the web a more ethical and user-friendly space.
